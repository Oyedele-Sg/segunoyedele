My Story

I am the C.E.O and Founder of Raiz Digital Services, a company setout to address the under-inclusion of Africans in global finance. I am very passionate about technology, finance, and software engineering and this has led me to embark on a journey of advocacy and solution creations for Africans.

My journey is marked by passion, dedication & unwavering commitment to creating impactful solutions.

Beyond professional achievements, awards, and accolades, I mentor aspiring tech enthusiasts, speak about financial and emotional well-being, leadership development, and I'm committed to nurturing the growth of the next generation of leaders.

Raiz

Raiz is a digital banking startup designed to simplify global banking for African individuals, freelancers, and businesses. With its flagship product, the Global Passport Account, Raiz enables users to hold, receive, convert, and send multiple currencies across 21+ countries without needing to leave their home country. This suite of offerings is enhanced by AI-powered personal financial advisors and tools to optimize financial decision-making.
